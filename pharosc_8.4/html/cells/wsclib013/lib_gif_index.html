Viewable GIF files for the wsclib. This release contains 319 inverting and non-inverting gates and 1 flip-flop. Each cell has a typical timing arc and input pin capacitance in 0.13µm generic technology, along with its leakage and dynamic power, size, layout, and the transistor schematic. Transistor sizes are given in lambda, where for this 0.13µm technology, lambda=0.055µm. The cell height is 80 lambda (4.4µm) with power supply rails 12 lambda (0.66µm) wide. The maximum P and N transistor widths before folding are 28λ and 20λ respectively.

The Graal layout is derived from the vsclib by a script, so the electrical characteristics of the library is the same. The difference is in the substrate and well contacts.
